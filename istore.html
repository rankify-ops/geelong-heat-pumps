Why iStore

Award-winning, app-controlled, ultra-efficient

The iStore is consistently our most-installed system — for good reason. It pairs serious efficiency with smart connectivity and quiet operation, all in a single all-in-one footprint that fits where your old electric tank used to live.

  • Large capacity: 270L tank suits households of 4–6 people (180L available on request)
  • Smart connectivity: Built-in WiFi lets you monitor and control settings via the iStore app
  • Quiet operation: Runs at approximately 47 dB for minimal noise disruption
