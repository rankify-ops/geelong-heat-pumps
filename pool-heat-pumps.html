Common Questions

Pool heat pump FAQs

How much does a pool heat pump cost to run?

It depends on pool size, target temperature, blanket use, and your electricity tariff — but inverter pool pumps typically run at a fraction of the cost of gas or electric resistance heating. Pair with solar and you can heat your pool for next to nothing.

How quickly will it heat my pool?

Heat-up time depends on pool volume and ambient temperature, but most residential pools reach swimming temperature within 24–48 hours from cold. A good thermal blanket roughly doubles efficiency.

Will it work in Geelong winters?

Yes — modern inverter pool pumps work efficiently down into the low single digits. You'll lose some swim season at the very coldest weeks, but most months are comfortably swimmable.

Do I need a thermal pool blanket?

We strongly recommend one. A blanket can halve your running costs by drastically reducing overnight heat loss and evaporation.
