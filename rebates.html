The Three Rebates

How Victorian homeowners stack savings

When you switch to a qualifying heat pump, you're eligible for up to three separate rebate programs. We're an approved installer for all of them.

🇺🇸

Federal STCs

Small-Scale Technology Certificates under the Renewable Energy Target. Value depends on system size, efficiency, and your zone — we trade the certificates and pass the value to you.

🏲

Victorian VEECs (VEU)

Victorian Energy Upgrades program rebates — designed to offset the cost of replacing inefficient hot water systems with high-efficiency heat pumps.

☀

Solar Victoria HW Rebate

Direct rebate from Solar Victoria for households upgrading to a heat pump or solar hot water system. Means-tested but generous when eligible.

FAQs

Rebate questions, answered

How much will I actually save?

It depends on your existing system, the heat pump you choose, your household income (for Solar Victoria), and your location. For most Geelong homes the combined value is between $1,400 and $2,500+. We give you the exact figure during your free quote.

Do I have to pay upfront and claim later?

No — we apply the rebate value as a discount on your invoice before you pay. You just pay the net amount.

Am I eligible if I'm renting?

Some rebates are available to renters with the property owner's consent. Get in touch — we'll check eligibility for your situation.

What is the Victorian gas phase-out?

From March 2027, Victorian homes must replace gas hot water systems with an efficient electric alternative when they reach end-of-life. Replacing now means highest rebates, no installer queues, and no stress when your old system fails.

Will the rebates last forever?

No — rebate values reduce over time as more homes switch. The federal STC value drops by roughly 1/15 each year. Acting sooner means a bigger rebate.
