Our Story

Built by tradies who care about Geelong

Geelong Heat Pumps was founded by Cam & Josh, two local tradies who saw families struggling with rising energy bills during the energy crisis — and knew there was a better way.

Cam brought over a decade of experience as an electrician specialising in renewable energy. Josh brought his plumbing expertise and a deep commitment to looking after customers properly.

Together, they set out to transform how Victorians access hot water — combining technical know-how with genuine local service.

  • Locally owned and operated — Newtown based
  • Licensed plumbers and electricians on every job
  • Approved installer for all major Victorian rebate schemes
  • 164+ five-star Google reviews
