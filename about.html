Our Story

Built by tradies who care about Geelong

Geelong Heat Pumps specialises in energy-efficient heat pump hot water systems across Geelong and Victoria. The business was founded by two best mates — an experienced plumber and an electrician with extensive knowledge of sustainable products — on a shared goal: helping local families reduce their energy bills while transitioning to cleaner technology.

With the rising cost of gas and electricity placing real pressure on Victorian households, heat pump hot water systems offer a highly efficient alternative. They use significantly less electricity than traditional electric or gas systems, helping homeowners lower running costs while reducing their environmental footprint.

Our focus is customer satisfaction, quality installations, and long-term sustainability — backed by a friendly team that guides customers through the entire process, from choosing the right system to securing every available Victorian rebate.
