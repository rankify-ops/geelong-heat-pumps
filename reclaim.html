Why Reclaim

Built for performance, designed for life

Engineered for modern living, the Reclaim runs more quietly (~37 dBA) than standard heat pumps and offers flexible options to suit your home — including stainless steel or glass-lined tanks and two compressor choices.

  • Up to 80% energy reduction vs traditional electric systems
  • High 110 L/hr recovery for busy households
  • Simple, smart control tailored to how you use hot water
  • Premium components — reliable performance
  • Built locally in Australia, designed to last
  • Whisper-quiet ~37 dB operation
Tank Options

Australian-made, four sizes

Designed and manufactured in Australia, Reclaim tanks are built for durability and performance across a range of household needs. Available in 160L, 250L, 315L, and 400L — there's a size to suit every home.

🛡

Stainless Steel

Ideal for standard water quality (non-high mineral or bore water). Excellent corrosion resistance and no anode replacement required — the low-maintenance option.

🧹

Glass Enamel

Suitable for all water types, including high mineral and bore water areas. Features a protective lining with a magnesium anode to be replaced every 3–5 years for ongoing performance.

Compressor Choice

Engineered in Japan

Reclaim heat pumps offer two high-quality Japanese compressor options — both proven, dependable, and built for reliable operation.

Panasonic

Compatible with all tank options and the V1.1 controller. A proven, dependable choice for efficient operation — the workhorse of the Reclaim range.

Chofu

Compatible with all tank options and both the V1.1 and V2 (WiFi) controllers — offering added flexibility and smart control capability.
