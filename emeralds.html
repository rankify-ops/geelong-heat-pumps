The Emerald range

Engineered efficiency, affordable price

Two 270L models with or without plug. Both share the same family-friendly capacity and 5-year parts & labour warranty — so the choice mostly comes down to compressor pedigree and recovery rate.

  • Emerald 270L Select — entry-level, with or without plug
  • Emerald 270L Pro — faster recovery, premium compressor
  • Both include built-in WiFi for smart control via app
  • Comprehensive 5-year parts and labour warranty
  • Fully eligible for the complete rebate stack
